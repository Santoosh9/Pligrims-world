Meeting/Conference/Incentives & Exhibitions

In the travel and tourism sector, package holidays—also referred to as tour packages or just "tours"—are tours that are either guided by a tour guide or are not. A pre-planned, pre-paid vacation that includes two or more travel components, such as flight, airport transportation, lodging, and other services, is referred to as a tour package. It is more difficult to define the tour package notion practically than it is to comprehend it. "A total tourism product consisting of transportation from the market area to the destination, accommodation at the destination, and recreational activities promoted by the tourists" is how Holloway describes a tour package. "A tour package is an advertised journey with specific features, organized and promoted with tour literature by a tour operator," according to Gregorg.
